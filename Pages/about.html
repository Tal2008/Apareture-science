A website about Apareture Science/Apareture Laboratories

Made by Tal

Aperture Science is a United States-based scientific research corporation appearing as the main antagonistic organization in the Portal and Portal 2 video games. They are the creators of the game's portal gun, which you use to solve puzzles and traverse through the levels.

I chose this as a topic for a website because why not? It's not like I had many ideas, and I like this game.
